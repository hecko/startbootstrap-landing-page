HotGlue Technologies, Edinburgh

Near-to-relatime Energy and Environmental monitoring

Energy and environmental factors monitoring:

HotGlueTech's electrical and environmental monitoring platform is a purpose-build, UK designed, developed and assembled equipment for energy and environmental data gathering and analysis with a goal to help save energy and increasing energy costs.

Shared goals:

In our highly vertically integrated monitoring system we are focusing on delivering two major answers to our custommers questions: How much power is individual electrical equipment using? and How much energy can I save by replacing or removing electrical equipment without negatively affecting my bussiness?
To deliver answers to these questions we not only monitor energy use, but all major environmental factors that might affect energy use - such as temperature (both exterior and interior) and environment light levels.

How does our system work?

Our system uses in-house developed electronics to gather and send data to datacentre on the Internet. Data are analyzed, corrrelated and translated using our analytics systems and presented to users - either in form of graphs and reports or as exported raw data for further analysis.
To monitor electricity use we are foremostly using non-invasive methods of sensing how much electrical power is delivered via individual wires coming from the fuse box. No electrician needed to install the monitoring solution. The data are transmitted to the Internet either using local computer network or mobile 3G network.

Data are streamed to the online service in a near-to-realtime fashion. If the Internet connection fails, the data are stored on a localy until the connection is restored.

To deliver high-precision power metering service for enterprise sector we employ MODBUS readers.

Wireless data transfer!

Our wireless sensors are designed to last years without replacing battery and can not only sense electricity use, but also environmental factors such as air temperature, barometric pressure, light-levels, sensing wind direction and wind speed. Noise-level monitoring and air quality sensors are currently being developed.
Sensors are designed for high efficiency and easy service.

About us and our processes:

We control every factor in the products life cycle and have designed the system from ground-up in our lab in Edinburgh. This means we can easily extend the platform to monitor many other environmental factors or specialized measurable events using the same unified system.
We control it all - firmware and software design and coding, hardware and PCB design; even writing our own software and programs to store and analyze data in the databases and presenting it to you! There is no external company we rely on when we develop our current portfolio or work on our future projects.
